Deviled Eggs

- Cook 6 eggs for 10 minutes. then cool and peel. - Halve eggs and remove yolks. - Mash yolks with 2 tbsp mayo, 1 tsp mustard, salt and pepper. - Spoon into whites, sprinkle with paprika.

Cucumber Bites

Stuffed Mushrooms

- Remove stems and clean caps. - Mix cream cheese, garlic, herbs, and breadcrumbs. - Fill caps witht the mixture. - Cook at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes.

Herb-Crusted Rack of Lamb

- Season rack with salt, pepper, and olive oil. - Mix garlic, rosemary,thyme,breadcrumbs, and mustard. - Spread herb mixture over the rack. - Cook at 400°F (200°C) for 20-25 minutes.

Beef Wellington

- Sear the beef tenderloin in hot oil until browned. Let it cool. - Cook mushrooms, garlic, and herbs until moisture evaporates. - Spread mustard pm beef , cover with duxelles, and wrap in prosciutto. - Roll beef in pastry, seal, and brush and egg wash. - Cook at 400°F (200°C) for 35-40 minutes.

Braised Short Ribs

- Brown the short ribs in hot oil on all sides, then set aside. - Saute onions, carrots, and garlic in the same pot. - Pour in beef brother, wine, and herbs; bring to a simmer. - Return ribs to the pot, cover, and braise at 325°F (160°C) for 2.5-3 hours. - Once tender, serve the ribs with sauce from the pot.

Crème Brûlée

- Set to 325°F (160°C). - Whisk egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla; heat cream until steaming, then mix into yolk mixture. - Pour custard into ramekins, place in a water bath, and bake for 40-45 minutes until set. - Cool in the fridge for at least 2 hours. - Sprinkle sugar on top and torch until golden.

Banana Foster

- In a pan, melt butter and brown sugar, Add sliced bananas and cook for 2-3 minutes. - Carefully pour in dark rum and banana liquer. Flame it. - Stir in cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla extract. - Pour sauce over vanilla ice cream and enjoy!

Honey and Ricotta Cheesecake

- Combine crushed graham crackers, melted butter, and a little sugar. Press into the bottom of the pan. - Beat Ricotta cheese, cream cheese, honey, eggs, and vanilla until smooth. - Pour mixture over the crust and bake at 325°F (165°C) for 40-45 minutes until set. - Let it cool, then refrigerate for a few hours. Drizzle with extra honey before serving.
